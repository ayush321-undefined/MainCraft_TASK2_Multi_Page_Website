The Story

From a basement to a global platform

Three engineers tired of duct-taping cloud services together started Nexus in 2014. The first version was a Bash script. Today, it orchestrates billions of requests a day for teams across six continents.

We've stayed private, stayed profitable, and stayed obsessed with one thing: making the technology that powers your product completely disappear from your daily consciousness.
